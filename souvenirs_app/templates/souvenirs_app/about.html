About GET A SOUVENIR

After the world faced a pandemic, a lot has changed in the life of every person. We all began to communicate less with each other, go to visit each other. And we began to travel less and bring souvenirs from other countries. But if we ourselves cannot bring a souvenir from another country, then why not get it from another person. The project is designed to help people exchange souvenirs. Send a souvenir to a random user from another country and expect to receive yours.

Rules:

Before sending a souvenir to a random recipient, please read the following information!

When you press the button "Send a souvenir", a person who doesn`t live in your country will be randomly selected. You will send your souvenir to this user, specifying a unique identifier. Get to know the person, learn their preferences, and send them a souvenir in a parcel. A souvenir that symbolizes your country. It can be some kind of delicacy, a commemorative postcard, a fridge magnet or something else.

Don't forget to attach a unique ID to the souvenir!

Remember that this project is for honest people. Please don't click send a souvenir if you're not going to do it!
